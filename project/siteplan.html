Website Site Plan

Discover Daily Need Distributors Ltd

Site Name

Discover Daily Need Distributors Ltd

This name represents a Nigerian distribution company that supplies daily-use consumer goods. The name was chosen because it reflects reliability, accessibility, and everyday essentials.

Optional domain availability: discoverdailyneed.com

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an online presence for Discover Daily Need Distributors Ltd. It will provide information about the company, the products distributed, contact details, and allow potential partners or customers to make inquiries.

Scenarios

  • What types of products does Discover Daily Need Distributors Ltd distribute?
  • How can I contact the company to become a distributor or supplier?

Color Schema

  • Primary Blue (#0D47A1): Used for headings and navigation.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used for background and clarity.
  • Accent Red (#C62828): Used for highlights and emphasis.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings and navigation.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text.
